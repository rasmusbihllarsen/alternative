Media Queries & breakpoints

Use the breakpoints to make your grid responsive. Just add the breakpoint as a prefix to the col.
If you want your div to be 50% on screens larger than 1400px, but 100% on smaller screens, add the classes: col-100 lg:col-50
Breakpoints:
xs: 0 - 767px
sm: 768px - 1024px
md: 1024px - 1400px
lg: 1400px +
md:15 sm:50
md:48 sm:25
sm:25 xs:50
md:12 xs:50
And it stretches!

Hide & Stealth

Theres two classes for hiding: .hide and .stealth.

Both .hide and .stealth can be used with breakpoints by prefixing with the breakpoint (fx. <div class="md:hide"></div>).

.hide is the hard-hide class, which means it gives a display: none; property to the element.

.stealth is the soft-hide class, and consists of opacity: 0; visibility: hidden; pointer-events: none;
